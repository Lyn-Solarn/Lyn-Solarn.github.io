Game Development

There are many components in Game Development that work together to create an enjoyable experience for the player. The following are brief introductions to their respective topics and what they are used to accomplish.

Graphics Rendering

How models, environments, and other visuals are "drawn" on the screen.

Procedural Content Generation (PCG)

Algorithms that can automatically generate terrain, levels, or textures.

Artificial Intelligence

How NPCs (Non-Player Characters) behave using path-finding, behavior trees, and other techniques.

Physics Engine

Ways to simulate physics to make movements, collisions, and interactions more realistic.
